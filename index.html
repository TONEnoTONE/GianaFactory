Lemon Moon is an interactive soundscape and mashup of Giana Factory's album Lemon Moon, produced by Anders Trentemøller and released by Fake Diamond Records May 2014.

Activate loops and samples from the album by running your mouse over the stars and constellations.

The visual interface is adapted from artist Andrea Galvani's album cover design. It is based on the stars as seen from the Libyan desert.

Lemon Moon is created in collaboration between Giana Factory, Louise Foo, Yotam Mann and Chris Deaner.
